# LanguageWire Neural MT

LanguageWire offers state-of-the-art Neural Machine Translation (NMT) engines, custom-trained for your specific industry and brand tone of voice.

## How it works

Unlike public translation tools, our NMT models are trained exclusively on your translation memories and glossaries. This ensures that the machine output sounds like your brand and adheres to your specific terminology.

## Continuous Learning

Our engines get smarter over time. Every time a human linguist edits a machine-translated segment (Post-Editing), the correction is fed back into the training loop. This means the engine will not make the same mistake twice.

## Supported Languages

We support over 100 language pairs with our Neural MT models. Popular pairs include:
- English to Danish, Swedish, and Norwegian
- English to German, French, and Spanish
- German to English

For languages with less data availability, we augment the training process using vast generic datasets before fine-tuning on your specific content.
