# About LanguageWire

LanguageWire is a leading technology-enabled language service provider. Founded in 2000, the company specializes in providing a full ecosystem for multilingual content creation, including translation, localization, and copywriting. 

By combining advanced artificial intelligence with a global network of language experts, LanguageWire helps global brands reach their target audiences in any language. Their platform streamlines the localization process, integrating directly with popular CMS and PIM systems to deliver secure and efficient content workflows.

Headquartered in Copenhagen, Denmark, LanguageWire has offices across Europe and the US, serving over 3,000 customers worldwide.
