# Security and Compliance at LanguageWire

Security is our top priority. When you translate confidential documents, you need to know your data is safe.

## Certifications

LanguageWire is fully certified according to the **ISO 27001** standard for information security management. We undergo regular third-party audits to ensure our controls meet the highest industry standards.

## Data Privacy (GDPR)

We are 100% compliant with the European General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Data is hosted in secure data centers located within the European Union (Germany and Denmark). We act as a Data Processor and offer standard Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) to all our enterprise customers.

## Confidentiality

- All data transmitted between your systems and LanguageWire is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3.
- Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256.
- Unlike free online translation tools, **we never use your data to train public models**, nor do we share your data with any third parties. Your translation memories and glossaries belong entirely to you.
